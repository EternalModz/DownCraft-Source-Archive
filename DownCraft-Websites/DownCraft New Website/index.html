Do you want to know more about DownCraft?
DownCraft is a simple RTM (Real Time Modding) working for support CFW & HEN. The RTM contains more than 200 options working on host / off host.

DownCraft have his own SPRX menu with all options available in the RTM, you can edit the design of the menu in the settings pages.
